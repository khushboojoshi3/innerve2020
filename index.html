ABOUT US

Innerve is the annual technical fest of Indira Gandhi Delhi Technical University for Women (IGDTUW). With its inception in 2015, every year, the two-day extravaganza has witnessed a phenomenol gathering of students from all domains. The event is back for its FIFTH edition where it aims to be a platform for participants to test their engineering skills and showcase their prowess.

Participation in events requires registration.Please navigate to the Events section for the same.
